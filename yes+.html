‘Su’ means proper, ‘darshan’ means vision, and ‘kriya’ is a purifying practice. Sudarshan Kriya is a purifying practice that gives a proper vision of one’s true self, or who one is. The practice is instrumental in creating a sense of harmony in the body, mind and spirit. There is a rhythm in Nature. Isn’t it? Just as seasons come and go, and days follow nights, our body too has its own rhythm. Have you heard of the biological clock? The biological clock is an internal system that controls the timing of organism behaviors, physiological states and processes. Did you know that there is also a rhythm to our thought process? Doubts, anxiety and happiness are related to a certain rhythm. You might have also observed that at a particular time in a year, you go through the same emotions. Sudarshan Kriya harmonizes the rhythms of the body and emotions and puts them back in tune with the rhythms of Nature.Being in rhythm, we feel good about ourselves. Love flows naturally in all relationships.The seven layers (body, mind, intellect, memory, ego, breath, and self or being) of our existence get harmonized.
